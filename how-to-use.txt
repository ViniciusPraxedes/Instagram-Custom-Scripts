HOW TO USE THE INSTAGRAM FOCUS SCRIPTS ON IPHONE

1. INSTALL THE EXTENSION
Download the "Userscripts" app from the Apple App Store.

2. ENABLE SAFARI PERMISSIONS
- Open "Settings" -> "Safari" -> "Extensions".
- Find "Userscripts" and switch it to "ON".

3. PREPARE THE SCRIPTS
- Open the "Files" app on your iPhone.
- Look for a folder named "Userscripts".
- Copy the following files into this folder:
   a) "reels.icon.remover.js" (Removes the Reels button)
   b) "instagram.content.cleaner.js" (Removes suggested content)
   c) "instagram.repost.remover.js" (Removes reposted posts)
   d) "instagram.following.switcher.js" (Ensures you are always on the "Following" feed)
   e) "instagram.collaborator.blocker.js" (Removes posts with multiple collaborators)

4. START BROWSING
- Open Safari and go to instagram.com.
- The Reels icon will be gone.
- Your home feed will only show original posts from accounts you follow.
- The "Repost" icon will be removed from the action bar under every post.

Enjoy your focused browsing experience!
